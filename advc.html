Master the Fold

The pinnacle of paper engineering. Patience and precision required.

Expert Level Required

Fiery Dragon

Level 10

A complex creature featuring over 50 individual scales and a detailed head structure.

  • Phase 1: Pre-crease a 32x32 grid to prepare for the scale sequence.
  • Phase 2: Collapse the bird base into a multi-pointed star shape.
  • Phase 3: Perform 24 inside-reverse folds for the neck and tail segments.
  • Phase 4: Use wet-folding techniques to shape the horns and claws.
  • Phase 5: Final shaping of the wings using crimp-folding layers.

Kawasaki Rose

Level 09

World-renowned for its mathematical beauty and realistic spiral petal structure.

  • Phase 1: Precise grid creasing using the Toshikazu Kawasaki method.
  • Phase 2: Develop the center "Twist Fold" without tearing the fibers.
  • Phase 3: Layer the outer petals using interlocking mountain folds.
  • Phase 4: Secure the base by tucking corners into the hidden internal slits.
  • Phase 5: Gently curl the outer edges using a toothpick for a natural look.

Grand Pegasus

Level 10+

Combines high-level biological accuracy with complex wing-span engineering.

  • Phase 1: Blintz-frog base collapse to isolate 8 primary points.
  • Phase 2: Double-sink folds to thin the legs and neck without bulk.
  • Phase 3: Pleat-folding the wing membrane for a feathered effect.
  • Phase 4: Elaborate head shaping including ears and a flowing mane.
  • Phase 5: Balancing the model's center of gravity for a standing pose.
